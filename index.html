How it works

AetherShare uses WebRTC technology to connect devices directly.

100% Private & Encrypted

Your files are sent directly between browsers using DTLS/SRTP encryption. No intermediate server store or cache.

Maximum Local Speeds

Transfer speeds are limited only by your internet or local network bandwidth. No artificial caps.

No Installation Required

Works entirely in the web browser. No accounts, no software, and no plugins to install.
